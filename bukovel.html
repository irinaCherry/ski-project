Bukovel

Bukovel is the largest ski resort in Eastern Europe located in Ukraine, Ivano-Frankivsk Oblast. The resort is located almost on the ridge-lines of the Carpathian Mountains at elevation of 900 m above the sea level. In 2012, Bukovel was named the fastest-growing ski resort in the world.
Bukovel currently boasts 17 ski lifts with roughly 60 km of pistes.
