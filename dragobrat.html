Dragobrat

Ski resort Dragobrat is the highest mountain ski resort in Ukraine, located at an altitude of 1400m, at the junction of coniferous forests and a zone of alpine meadows at the foot of Mount Stog (1707m) near the mountain range Bliznitsa (1883m) in the Zakarpattia Oblast. Natural snow cover is guaranteed because of the considerable height and unique climate.

For skiing and snowboarding, there are 10 km of slopes available. 13 lifts transport the guests.
